TORQ-e Provider Guide

Streamline Your Claims and Enrollment

What is TORQ-e for Providers?

TORQ-e is a powerful tool that saves healthcare providers time and money by automating claims processing, enrollment verification, and fraud detection. Instead of juggling multiple portals and waiting days for answers, you get real-time insights about member coverage, claim validity, and payment status.

Think of TORQ-e as your personal claims assistant that's available 24/7.

What Can TORQ-e Do For You?

  • Look up your current Medicaid enrollment status (FFS, MCO, or both)
  • Verify member coverage before you submit a claim
  • Validate a claim against member eligibility and coverage limits BEFORE you submit it
  • Submit claims to the right place automatically (FFS vs. MCO routing)
  • Track claim status and payment timelines in real-time
  • Get alerts if a claim is delayed past the 30-day federal payment deadline
  • Analyze a claim for fraud patterns and red flags (unusual billing, overutilization, etc.)
  • Catch billing errors early and resubmit faster

What Can't TORQ-e Do?

  • TORQ-e cannot process payments or send money directly to your account
  • TORQ-e cannot enroll you in Medicaid—you still apply through official channels
  • TORQ-e cannot appeal a denied claim (you need to follow denial procedures)
  • TORQ-e cannot modify member eligibility or add/remove coverage levels
  • TORQ-e does not work with non-Medicaid insurance (Medicare, commercial plans, etc.)
  • TORQ-e cannot override Medicaid rules or exceptions set by DOH
  • For major account issues or manual exceptions, you still need to contact Medicaid directly

How It Works

Upload your claim or member info and ask TORQ-e questions: "Is this member covered?" or "Is this claim valid?" TORQ-e checks multiple data sources and flags issues before they become problems. You get a confidence score with every answer, so you know when to take extra care.

Most claims can be validated in seconds, not hours.
