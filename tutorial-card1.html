TORQ-e Member Guide

Understanding Your Medicaid Coverage

What is TORQ-e for Members?

TORQ-e is a simple tool that helps you understand your Medicaid coverage instantly. Instead of waiting hours on the phone, you can ask TORQ-e about your eligibility, coverage dates, income changes, and benefits—and get answers in seconds.

Whether you're a Member, Client, Recipient, or Beneficiary, TORQ-e is here to help you stay informed about your Medicaid status.

What Can TORQ-e Do For You?

  • Check if you're covered by Medicaid right now
  • See your coverage dates and when your coverage expires
  • Find out when you need to renew or recertify your eligibility
  • Report a change in your income and see if you're still eligible
  • Upload documents (ID, pay stubs, proof of address) to support your application
  • Track your application or eligibility status in real-time
  • Get explanations in plain language—no confusing jargon

What Can't TORQ-e Do?

  • TORQ-e cannot enroll you in Medicaid—you still apply through the official Medicaid office
  • TORQ-e cannot process benefit payments or send you money
  • TORQ-e cannot change your coverage or add dependents (you need to contact Medicaid directly)
  • TORQ-e cannot schedule appointments for you
  • TORQ-e cannot access information about private insurance or other benefits
  • If you need emergency assistance or have a critical issue, call the Medicaid office instead

How It Works

Simply chat with TORQ-e. Tell it your situation: "I just got a raise, am I still covered?" or "When do I need to recertify?" TORQ-e checks multiple sources of information and gives you the most accurate answer it can find, along with a confidence score so you know how certain we are.

If TORQ-e isn't sure about something, it will tell you and suggest the next step.
