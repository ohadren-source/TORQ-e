What Will TORQ-e Offer Government Stakeholders?

TORQ-e is being built to provide government agencies with comprehensive program oversight, compliance monitoring, performance metrics, and regulatory reporting across the entire Medicaid system. This will enable DOH and other stakeholders to ensure program integrity and regulatory compliance.

Planned Features

  • Real-time program oversight and monitoring dashboards
  • Compliance tracking and audit reporting
  • Performance metrics and key indicator tracking
  • Regulatory reporting (federal and state)
  • System-wide analytics and trend analysis
  • Alerting for compliance issues or anomalies
  • Historical reporting and trend visualization

What It Won't Do

  • TORQ-e cannot modify federal or state Medicaid rules
  • TORQ-e cannot override individual eligibility determinations
  • TORQ-e cannot process benefit payments or enrollments
  • TORQ-e cannot enforce compliance—that remains a DOH function
  • TORQ-e is not a replacement for formal audit and inspection processes
