What Will TORQ-e Offer Data Analysts?

TORQ-e is being built to provide authenticity investigators and data analysts with powerful tools for pattern detection, anomaly identification, trend analysis, and case management. This will enable your team to identify and investigate potential inauthenticity, waste, and abuse across the entire Medicaid system.

Planned Features

  • Advanced authenticity pattern detection (billing anomalies, over-utilization, etc.)
  • Anomaly detection and outlier identification
  • Trend analysis across providers, members, and services
  • Case management and investigation workflow tools
  • Data export for deeper analysis and reporting
  • Automated alerting for high-risk patterns
  • Investigative report generation
  • Historical tracking of known bad actors and patterns

What It Won't Do

  • TORQ-e cannot initiate enforcement actions or prosecutions
  • TORQ-e cannot make final inauthenticity determinations (that's your team's role)
  • TORQ-e cannot recover funds or recoup payments
  • TORQ-e is not a substitute for human investigation and case review
  • TORQ-e cannot override due process or legal procedures
  • TORQ-e findings require human verification before action

How It Works

TORQ-e uses signal processing to identify patterns and anomalies in claims, member usage, and provider billing. You upload data or ask specific questions, and TORQ-e surfaces suspicious cases ranked by risk level. Your team investigates, verifies, and takes appropriate action.

Think of TORQ-e as your pattern-finding microscope that helps you focus on high-risk cases.
