SECONDARY QUESTION: "How do I send in a claim?"

User Types This Into Text Box:
  "How do I send in a claim?"

Reading Engine Process:
  1. Check if provider is enrolled
  2. Retrieve claims submission requirements
  3. Verify provider is not in claims suspension
  4. Provide step-by-step claim submission guide
  5. Claude AI explains process

Example Response:
  "Here's how to submit a Medicaid claim:

   STEP 1: Verify Patient Eligibility
   • Go to EMEDNY portal
   • Search patient by name/Medicaid ID
   • Confirm Medicaid is active

   STEP 2: Gather Required Documentation
   • Service date
   • Patient Medicaid ID
   • Procedure codes (CPT/HCPCS)
   • Diagnosis codes (ICD-10)
   • Units of service

   STEP 3: Submit Claim
   • Use EMEDNY electronic claims portal
   • OR send paper form CMS-1500
   • Include all supporting documentation

   STEP 4: Track Claim Status
   • Claims typically process in 15-30 days
   • Check status in EMEDNY portal
   • Request denied claims review if needed

   [Access EMEDNY] [Download Form] [Get Help]"

8. PROVIDER FAQ RESPONSES
================================================================================

FAQ 1: "Which services require prior approval before I bill?"

Claude Response + Reading Engine:
  - Retrieve prior approval list from Medicaid policies
  - Filter by provider specialty and service type
  - Show: High-cost services, experimental treatments, etc.

Display Format:
  "These services require PRIOR APPROVAL before billing:

   HIGH-COST PROCEDURES:
   ✓ Imaging (MRI, CT beyond 2/year)
   ✓ Joint Replacement Surgery
   ✓ Certain Medications (specialty pharma)
   ✓ Home Health (more than X visits)

   SPECIALIZED SERVICES:
   ✓ Behavioral Health Programs
   ✓ Dialysis
   ✓ Durable Medical Equipment (high-value)
   ✓ Out-of-State Treatment

   HOW TO REQUEST PRIOR APPROVAL:
   1. Go to EMEDNY portal
   2. Submit Prior Authorization request
   3. Wait for approval (typically 5 business days)
   4. Once approved, submit claim

   [Submit Prior Auth Request] [Check Status]"

---

FAQ 2: "How do I verify that Medicaid is the primary payer?"

Claude Response + Reading Engine:
  - Explain primary/secondary insurance rules
  - Provide verification steps
  - Show coordination of benefits rules

Display Format:
  "Medicaid is the PRIMARY payer if:
   • Patient has Medicaid only
   • Patient is over 65 with Medicare + Medicaid (Dual-eligible)
   • Patient has COBRA or other employer coverage (Medicaid 2ndary)

   TO VERIFY MEDICAID AS PRIMARY:
   1. Go to EMEDNY portal
   2. Search patient by Medicaid ID
   3. Check 'Insurance Status' section
   4. Confirm 'Primary Insurance: Medicaid'

   If patient has OTHER insurance:
   • Bill other insurance FIRST
   • Bill Medicaid for remaining balance
   • This is Coordination of Benefits (COB)

   COORDINATION OF BENEFITS (COB) Steps:
   1. Collect patient's other insurance info
   2. Bill other insurance first
   3. Get Explanation of Benefits (EOB)
   4. Bill Medicaid for remaining balance
   5. Medicaid pays up to their allowed amount

   [Check Patient Eligibility] [Submit Coordination Claim]"

---

FAQ 3: "How long does it take to get paid after submitting a claim?"

Claude Response + Reading Engine:
  - Retrieve average claim processing times
  - Explain clean vs. problematic claims
  - Show claim status tracking

Display Format:
  "Standard Claim Processing Timeline:

   CLEAN CLAIMS (no issues):
   • Electronic submission: 15-30 days
   • Paper submission: 20-35 days
   • Payment method: Direct deposit or check

   PROBLEMATIC CLAIMS (missing info, errors):
   • Initial review: 5 days
   • Notification to provider: 10 days
   • Corrected claim processing: Additional 15-30 days

   HOW TO TRACK YOUR CLAIM:
   1. Go to EMEDNY portal
   2. Select 'Claim Status'
   3. Search by claim number or date range
   4. View status: Received, Approved, Denied, Paid

   TO SPEED UP PAYMENT:
   ✓ Submit electronically (faster than paper)
   ✓ Verify patient eligibility before billing
   ✓ Use correct procedure/diagnosis codes
   ✓ Include all required documentation
   ✓ Submit within 30 days of service

   AVERAGE PAYMENT AMOUNT: $[varies by service]
   PAYMENT METHOD: Direct Deposit (recommended)

   [Track My Claims] [Update Banking Info] [Request Payment Status]"
