3. PLAN JOURNEY - MAIN QUESTIONS
================================================================================

PRIMARY QUESTION: "Is this provider in my network? Out of network?"

User (Member or Plan Staff) Types:
  "Is Dr. Jane Smith in my network?"
  OR "Is this address in-network?"
  OR "Check provider network status"

Reading Engine Process:
  1. Parse provider name/NPI/address from question
  2. Retrieve plan's current network directory
  3. Cross-reference provider against in-network list
  4. Check provider credentials (UPID verification)
  5. Determine contract status and copay difference
  6. Claude AI synthesizes response

Example Response:
  "DR. JANE SMITH - PEDIATRICIAN

   ✓ IN NETWORK
   • Location: 123 Main St, New York, NY
   • Specialty: Pediatrics
   • Accepting new patients: Yes
   • Copay: $0 (preventive), $15 (visit)

   WHAT THIS MEANS:
   • No prior authorization needed
   • Use your member copay ($0-15)
   • Bill goes to our plan directly
   • You can schedule appointment

   [Call Provider] [Schedule Appointment]
   [View Other Pediatricians in Area]"

SECONDARY QUESTION 1: "What are the out-of-pocket costs (copayments, deductibles)?"

User Types:
  "What are my copayments?"
  OR "Do I have a deductible?"
  OR "How much does a doctor visit cost?"

Reading Engine Process:
  1. Retrieve plan's benefit structure
  2. Identify member's eligibility category
  3. Pull copay/deductible schedule
  4. Categorize by service type
  5. Claude AI explains cost-sharing

Example Response:
  "YOUR OUT-OF-POCKET COSTS (2026):

   PRIMARY CARE:
   • Doctor visit: $15 copay
   • Preventive care: $0 copay
   • Behavioral health: $15 copay

   SPECIALIST CARE:
   • Specialist visit (with referral): $25 copay
   • Specialist visit (without referral): 40% coinsurance

   HOSPITAL & ER:
   • Emergency room: $250 copay (waived if admitted)
   • Hospital stay: $0 copay
   • Urgent care: $50 copay

   PRESCRIPTIONS:
   • Generic drugs: $1 copay
   • Brand drugs: $15 copay
   • Specialty drugs: 25% coinsurance

   DEDUCTIBLE:
   • Individual: $0 (this plan has no deductible)
   • Family: N/A

   OUT-OF-POCKET MAXIMUM:
   • Individual: $5,000/year
   • Family: $10,000/year

   [See Full Coverage Details] [Download Benefits Card]"

SECONDARY QUESTION 2: "How do I assign beneficiaries? Make changes? See current plan details?"

User Types:
  "How do I add a family member?"
  OR "Change my plan"
  OR "See my current coverage"

Reading Engine Process:
  1. Identify if user is plan admin or member
  2. If member: Show personal enrollment status
  3. If plan admin: Show bulk assignment tools
  4. Provide step-by-step for changes
  5. Claude AI guides through process

  - Deductibles
  - Coinsurance percentages
  - Out-of-pocket maximums

ENROLLMENT INFORMATION:

  - Service areas (counties/regions)
  - Plan type (HMO, PPO, MLTC)
  - Open enrollment dates
  - Special enrollment periods

5. PLAN FAQ RESPONSES
================================================================================

FAQ 1: "Do beneficiaries need a referral from their primary care physician to see a specialist?"

Claude Response:
  "REFERRAL REQUIREMENTS:

   IF YOU'RE AN HMO MEMBER:
   ✓ YES - You need a referral for specialists

   HOW IT WORKS:
   1. Choose a primary care doctor (PCP)
   2. Call PCP's office to schedule
   3. Tell PCP you need to see a specialist
   4. PCP submits referral to us
   5. We send referral to specialist
   6. You can then schedule with specialist

   TIME FOR REFERRAL: 1-2 business days

   WITHOUT REFERRAL:
   × You'll pay 40% coinsurance instead of $25 copay
   × May not be covered at all

   EXCEPTIONS (No referral needed):
   ✓ Pediatricians (if you have kids)
   ✓ OB/GYN (women can self-refer)
   ✓ Emergency services
   ✓ Out-of-network (emergency)

   [Request Referral] [Find PCP] [Contact Plan]"

FAQ 3: "Can beneficiaries switch plans or change providers mid-year?"

Claude Response:
  "SWITCHING PLANS:

   DURING ANNUAL OPEN ENROLLMENT (Oct 15 - Dec 7):
   ✓ You can switch to any plan
   ✓ New coverage starts January 1
   ✓ No restrictions

   OUTSIDE OPEN ENROLLMENT:
   ✗ Cannot switch (with rare exceptions)

   LIFE EVENTS (Allowed mid-year):
   ✓ Marriage/domestic partnership
   ✓ Divorce
   ✓ Birth/adoption
   ✓ Job loss (COBRA loss)
   ✓ Loss of other coverage
   ✓ Death in family
   ✓ Relocation (moving out of service area)

   HOW TO SWITCH:
   1. Report life event to NY State of Health
   2. You'll get 60 days to choose new plan
   3. New coverage starts within 1-2 months

   CHANGING DOCTORS WITHIN YOUR PLAN:
   ✓ You can change your PCP anytime
   ✓ Most specialists can be changed with referral
   ✓ Call your plan to request change
   ✓ Effective immediately for new care

   [Report Life Event] [Switch Plan] [Change PCP]"
