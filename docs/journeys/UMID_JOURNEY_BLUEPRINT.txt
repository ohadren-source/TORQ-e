7. MEMBER JOURNEY - MAIN QUESTION
================================================================================

MAIN QUESTION: "Am I eligible for Medicaid?"

User Types This Into Text Box:
  "Am I eligible for Medicaid?"

Reading Engine Process:
  1. Extract key information from member's UMID/profile
     - Age
     - Household size
     - Monthly income
     - Citizenship status
     - Current employment

  2. Run eligibility algorithm against NYS Medicaid rules
     - Check Modified Adjusted Gross Income (MAGI) limits
     - Check categorical eligibility (age, disability, pregnancy)
     - Check citizenship requirements
     - Determine eligibility category (if eligible)

  3. Claude AI synthesizes response
     - "Yes, you are eligible for Medicaid because..."
     - "No, your income exceeds the limit. Here's what to do..."
     - "Your eligibility depends on: [factor]. Can you confirm?"

  4. Display answer + FAQ suggestions

Example Response:
  "Based on your household size (3) and monthly income ($2,100),
   you qualify for Medicaid. Your eligibility category is:
   MAGI Non-Pregnant Adult. Benefits begin immediately after
   enrollment confirmation.

   Learn more:
   • What services does Medicaid cover? [View]
   • How long does coverage last? [View]
   • How do I use my member ID? [View]"

8. MEMBER FAQ RESPONSES
================================================================================

FAQ 1: "What services and treatments does Medicaid cover?"

Claude Response + Reading Engine:
  - Pull from NYS Medicaid covered services list
  - Filter by member's eligibility category
  - Show: Primary care, specialists, hospital, emergency, prescription drugs
  - Show: Mental health, dental (if eligible), vision
  - Show: Preventive care (free)
  - Show: Cost-sharing rules (copays, deductibles)

Display Format:
  "Medicaid covers these services:

   ✓ Primary Care Doctor Visits
   ✓ Specialist Visits (with referral if HMO member)
   ✓ Hospital Care
   ✓ Emergency Services
   ✓ Prescription Drugs
   ✓ Mental Health Services
   ✓ Preventive Care (free)

   Your out-of-pocket costs:
   • Doctor visit: $0-$3 copay
   • Prescription: $1-$3 copay
   • Hospital: $0 copay

   [Need something not listed? Ask your doctor about prior approval]"

---

FAQ 2: "How do I renew my Medicaid eligibility?"

Claude Response + Reading Engine:
  - Check member's renewal date (from eligibility database)
  - Provide step-by-step renewal instructions
  - Show: When to renew, how to renew, what information needed
  - Alert: If renewal due soon

Display Format:
  "Your Medicaid eligibility renews on: June 30, 2026

   30 days before: You'll receive renewal notice by mail + email

   To renew, you can:
   1. Online: Go to NY State of Health (nystateofhealth.ny.gov)
   2. By phone: Call 1-855-355-5777
   3. By mail: Return the renewal form you receive

   You'll need to provide:
   • Current household income
   • Employment status
   • Changes in family composition
   • Updated address/contact info

   [Renew now] [Set reminder] [Get help]"

Claude Response + Reading Engine:
