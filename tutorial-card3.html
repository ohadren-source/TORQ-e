What Will TORQ-e Offer Plan Administrators?

TORQ-e is being built to help health plan administrators manage provider networks, configure plan parameters, monitor network adequacy, and oversee day-to-day plan operations. This tool will enable plan managers to optimize network performance and ensure regulatory compliance.

Planned Features

  • Manage provider networks and credentialing status
  • Configure plan parameters and coverage rules
  • Monitor network adequacy and geographic coverage
  • Oversee plan operations and member enrollment
  • Generate compliance reports for regulators
  • Track performance metrics and key indicators

What It Won't Do

  • TORQ-e cannot override state or federal Medicaid regulations
  • TORQ-e cannot process member eligibility determinations (that's UMID's role)
  • TORQ-e cannot handle individual claim disputes or appeals
  • TORQ-e cannot modify federal payment rates or reimbursement schedules
