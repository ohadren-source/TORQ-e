How to Sign Up for Medicaid

Get covered in 4 simple steps

How to Sign Up

  1. Go to the marketplace: Visit nystateofhealth.ny.gov
  2. Provide your information: Income, household size, citizenship status, and basic personal details
  3. Pick a plan: Choose from available health plans
  4. You're covered: Coverage starts, and you'll receive confirmation by mail

Free Help Available

Call 1-855-355-5777 for free assistance with your application.

Or visit your local Department of Social Services (DSS) office.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay to apply? ▼
No. There is no cost to apply for Medicaid. It's completely free.
Can I apply anytime? ▼
Yes. You can apply for Medicaid any time of the year. There's no deadline.
How long does it take to get coverage? ▼
You'll receive a decision letter in the mail. Coverage can be retroactive up to 3 months before you apply.
What documents do I need? ▼
You'll need proof of income, residency, and citizenship/immigration status. Specific documents depend on your situation. The marketplace will tell you what to bring.
What if I'm unsure about my eligibility? ▼
That's okay. Apply anyway. The system will tell you if you qualify. Call 1-800-541-2831 if you have questions.
I already have coverage. Can I still apply? ▼
Yes. You can switch to Medicaid anytime if you qualify. Just apply and let the system compare your options.
